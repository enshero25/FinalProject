What Is Industrial Insulation?

Insulation in refineries helps industrial plants reduce energy costs, making chemical processing more efficient. Insulation also protects staff from workplace risks including burns and noise pollution. Because of this, insulation for refineries may also reduce the liability of refinery owners.

Types of Insulation...

Choosing the right type of insulation — and the perfect supplier to help you along the way — is crucial when insulating any type of industrial setting, though especially refineries. Without proper industrial-grade insulation, you can put yourself and employees at risk of hearing damage, burns, and more. Not to mention, equipment and pipes will have a longer lifespan if they are fitted with proper insulation. This means more money in thier pocket.

Tools and Equipment

There are a variety of tools required to properly install insulation such as, Measuring Tape, Snips, Knifes, Saws, Bander/Tensioner, Dividers/Compass, Scissors, and Nippers. If you are just starting you can spend around $500 just in the basic tools to install material as well as the metal jacketing that goes over the insulation. Aside from the tools it is also very important to always wear the PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) provided to you.

Personal Protective Equipment

Usually, Asbestos Personal Protective Equipment is required on older sites due to older insulation materials possibly containing Asbestos. Newer facilities sometimes do not require respirators; just a face covering will suffice. When installing Metal Jacketing it is important to wear Kevlar Sleeves to protect against sharp metal edges.
