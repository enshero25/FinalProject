Procedure?

Straight Pipe Install



Foamglass is an insulation that is commonly known in liquid nitrogen or cold temp pipe. Aside from the black grainy material it also requires that no gaps in between pieces and sealent is used to make sure of this. One it is secures with fiber tape or metal bands, a vapor sealent wrap is installed over the material. It sticks on the grainy surface and provides an additional seal to prevent snowballs or ice to damage pipe.

90° Elbow Install



This step is very similar to the straight pipe method. The insulation installed and secured with tape or metal bands. After that the wrap that provides additional seal is patterned and cut into gores that make up the 90 degree elbow in order to complete the installation. At times it may be necessary to carve into the material for a snugg fit around the pipe. Soon after everything is properly installed, the metal jacketing can be applied.
