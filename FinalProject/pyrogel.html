Straight Pipe Install



Any straight pipe insulation must be cut to appropriate length and squared off at the start to ensure a straight even cut. A piece of scrap insulation can also be used to measure length and cut accordingly. It must be installed in sections of 3ft width and staggered when more than one layer is to be installed. As seen on the above once placed on the pipe wrap the material around the pipe and secure with stainless steel wire or fiber tape tightly.

90° Elbow Install



Pyrogel is such a flexibles material compared to others that I really do enjoy installing it. Another plus is that usually can be ordered pre-fabricated and already precisely cut to fit specification. On smaller diameter piping, the bow-like cut is used and just requires you to wrap insulation around 90 degree elbow and secure it with tape or wire. On bigger pipes individual gores are installed individually around the elbow joint.
